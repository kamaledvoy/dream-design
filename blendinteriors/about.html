Blend Design studio

Blend Design Studio is a Chennai based architectural and interior design firm. The studio was found in 2018 to set the sense of simplicity in par with intricacy and bring out the most aesthetically stunning buildings. Blend Design Studio has a team of the youngest yet the finest designers to bestow upon our clients our eccentric ideas and designs. Starting small, our first project was a café, since then we have progressively extended our venture through all types of commercial and residential projects.

Our firm’s portfolio is the collection of projects that showcases each of our project’s uniqueness in design and material yet provide a welcoming and warm feel. Blend Design Studio has set its own trademark by styling each project sensibly with the best space management technique to make the best use of it.

We design in a synergetic environment enabling us to satisfy our client’s objective. Our designs are current, yet they possess a timeless style. Our designers are forever in search of the latest design styles and materials to unveil the best out of the spaces.

We take pride in our work ethic, our eye for innovative designs, and our allegiance to our client.
